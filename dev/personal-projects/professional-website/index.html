About Me

Hi! My name is Philip. I am a detail oriented software engineer passionate about learning.

I have experience developing, supporting, testing, and writing specifications for an Enterprise Retail Software application, specifically in the retail furnishing space.

I have worked on full stack projects in the Point of Sale, Inventory Management, EDI, Reporting, Trigger Architecture, and Credit Card Processing modules within the application.

I dedicate time daily to further my knowledge about software development & architecture by reading books, listening to podcasts, and working on side projects.
